Pyramid Schemes

All the apps listed below I have on my phone and use regularly. Each link will direct you to Sign Up using my name as a reference.

  1. Robinhood is a basic investing app that is completely commision free. I joined the app to learn a little about the stock market with low risk. While I may only have $20 invested, I have friends who have invested thousands. Whether you're looking for just a small place to start or are serious about investing, Robinhood makes it simple.
  2. Acorns is an investing app,owned by Paypal, but you invest in a much different way than robinhood. Acorns invests for you with options on how aggressively you want to invest. The app also has an interesting feature that automatically rounds up any purchases you make, on selected banking cards, to collect "virtual change" that goes into your investing. This allows you to create a more passive income off of subtle savings made every time you make a purchase. Full disclaimer I have not started using this application yet due to the fact that I lack any income while at college, but it is a product I genuinely hope to start using shortly. The app is free for students and costs a dollar a month otherwise
  3. SweatCoin is quite honestly the most click-batey type of apps out there. The whole concept of the app is that it tracks how many steps you take and turns them into their own fake currency. Their fake currency can be redeemed for actual things like paypal cash. The app is cluttered with ads and they're probably selling my data for a profit somewhere. Reguardless, the app runs in the background and doesn't seem to affect anything. It also allows you to transfer the fake currency to friends so if anyone wants to pull together our fake currencys to buy something let me know
